FAQ

What should I bring?

Comfortable hiking shoes, water, sun protection, and a positive attitude.

Is this family friendly?

Yes! All ages are welcome. The trail is beginner-friendly.

What happens if it rains?

The event will proceed in light rain. In the case of severe weather, we'll contact you with updates.
